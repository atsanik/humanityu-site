Scientific Integrity

Real data.
Real impact.

Every number on the leaderboard comes from real user input through the in-app calculators — not estimates, AI guesses, or pre-filled values. Entries are self-reported.

Calculator-only metrics — gallons, CO₂, trees. Gamification never inflates scientific scores.

Data attribution — all statistics sourced from NOAA, WHO, NASA, UN, FAO. Full reference log in-app.

Offline resilience — failed submissions queue locally and auto-retry when connectivity restores.

Crisis zone alerts — when a critical crisis worsens in your region, you receive an immediate push notification.
