Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026  ·  Effective: June 7, 2026

1Introduction

HumanityU ("we," "us," or "our") is a scientific-grade environmental impact tracking platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the HumanityU mobile application (iOS and Android) and related services (the "Service").

By using HumanityU, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Our commitment: HumanityU does not sell your personal data, we do not use it for advertising, and we do not share it with data brokers. Your environmental measurements belong to you.

2Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account: Email address (authentication only) and Username (display name on leaderboards and community posts).

2.2 Environmental Measurements

When you use calculators, we store measurements you enter (gallons saved, kg CO₂ reduced, trees planted) to build your impact record and leaderboard ranking.

2.3 Location Data (Coarse — Optional)

If you grant location permission, GPS is used once to determine your country and conservation region — then immediately discarded. We store only country name, continent, and region name. No precise GPS coordinates are ever stored. Location is optional — you can set your region manually in Profile at any time.

2.4 User-Generated Content

When posting to the Community feed, v1 supports text posts and comments only. Text posts are automatically screened before publication. Photo and video uploads are disabled until moderated media review ships.

2.5 Usage Data

Minimal technical data: app error logs (crash reports) and AI feature usage metrics for service health monitoring. We do not collect device fingerprints, advertising IDs, browsing history, or cross-app tracking data.

3How We Use Your Data

  • App functionality — authenticating your account, displaying your impact record, leaderboard rankings, and community feed.
  • Regional leaderboards — grouping users by country and conservation region.
  • Content moderation — AI-assisted pre-screening of community posts and comments. Post content is processed transiently and not retained by the AI provider.
  • Push notifications — streak reminders, milestone alerts, and weekly digests. Disable in Profile → Notification Settings at any time.
  • Offline sync — temporarily caching failed submissions in device local storage for automatic retry.
  • App improvement — anonymous aggregate error logs.

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, selling to third parties, or any purpose not listed above.

4Third-Party Services

Supabase
Database · Authentication

Powers our backend database (PostgreSQL) and user authentication (email + OTP). Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. It is not end-to-end encrypted — your community content is processed by our systems for safety moderation and app functionality, and is readable by authorized infrastructure. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II compliant.

Supabase Privacy Policy →
Owner-Controlled AI Gateway
AI Content Moderation · Conservation Advisor · Crisis News Curation

Provides large language model capabilities for community moderation, the Conservation Advisor Q&A, and crisis news curation through the configured owner-controlled gateway. Text is processed transiently and not retained beyond the immediate request. No personal account data is shared.

OpenTopoMap / OpenStreetMap
Map Tile Rendering

The Crisis Map tab loads topographic tiles from OpenTopoMap servers. Tile requests include approximate viewport coordinates. No personal identifying information is transmitted. Data is available under CC-BY-SA 3.0.

Apple APNs / Google FCM
Push Notifications

Push notifications are delivered via Apple APNs (iOS) and Google FCM (Android). Your push token is stored solely for delivering notifications you have opted into. Revoke at any time via device settings or Profile → Notification Settings.

5Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only with sub-processors (Section 4), as required by law, or in a business transfer (with notice and policy continuity guaranteed).

Public information: Your username, conservation region, and environmental metrics are publicly visible on the leaderboard and community feed by design. Your email address is never publicly visible.

7Security

  • All data in transit encrypted with TLS 1.2+
  • Data at rest encrypted by Supabase managed infrastructure
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users access only their own data
  • Authentication uses one-time passwords (OTP) delivered to verified email addresses

8Children's Privacy

HumanityU is intended for users aged 13 and older. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

HumanityU is rated for a teen audience because it includes user-generated content (community posts and comments). Direct messaging is disabled in this version. We collect only email, username, and optional coarse (country/region) location — no behavioral tracking, no advertising, and no in-app purchases or monetization of any kind. Text posts are automatically screened before publication; photo and video uploads are disabled until moderated media review ships. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information, contact atsanik.llc@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

9Your Privacy Rights

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — update inaccurate data via Profile or by contacting us
  • Deletion — request full account deletion (Section 6.2)
  • Portability — request export of your environmental measurement data
  • Withdraw consent — revoke location or notification permissions via device settings

California (CCPA): You have rights to know, delete, and opt out of sale. We do not sell personal information.

EU/EEA (GDPR): Legal basis is performance of contract. You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10Cookies & Tracking Technologies

HumanityU is a mobile application and does not use browser cookies. The app uses AsyncStorage (device-local only) for onboarding state, offline submission queue, and notification preferences. This data is never transmitted to third parties. We do not use advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or any third-party tracking.

11International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States and European Union where our service providers maintain infrastructure. For EEA users, transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as required by GDPR Article 46.
