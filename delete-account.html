Delete Your HumanityU Account

You can permanently delete your HumanityU account and the personal data associated with it at any time. This page explains how, and what is removed.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open HumanityU and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Your account is deleted immediately and the data below is removed.

Option 2 — By email

Email support@humanityu.app with the subject Delete My Account from the email address on your account. We will permanently delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

What is deleted

Your account record, email, username/display name, profile and conservation region, your environmental measurements and journals, your community posts and comments, your uploaded media, and your push-notification token.

What may be retained, and for how long

We may retain a limited record where the law requires it (for example, to comply with legal obligations or to resolve disputes), and anonymized, non-identifying error logs for up to 90 days. Retained data is not used to re-identify you.
